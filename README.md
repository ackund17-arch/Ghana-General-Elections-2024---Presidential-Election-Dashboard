# Ghana-General-Elections-2024---Presidential-Election-Dashboard


An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Ghana's 2024 Presidential Election results, comparing the performance of the two major candidates across all regions using KPIs, dynamic filters, and insightful visualizations.


Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Ghana's 2024 Presidential Election results, focusing on the two leading political parties. The dashboard enables users to compare candidate performance across all sixteen regions, visualize voting patterns, and explore regional voting distributions through dynamic charts and filters.

The objective was to transform raw election results into an intuitive business intelligence dashboard that allows users to quickly identify voting trends, regional strongholds, and overall election outcomes.

Business Problem

Election data often contains thousands of records spread across multiple regions, making it difficult to identify patterns and compare candidate performance efficiently. Stakeholders such as journalists, researchers, policymakers, and citizens require a centralized and interactive reporting solution that simplifies election analysis.

Project Objectives

Analyze the 2024 Ghana Presidential Election results.
Compare vote shares between the two major presidential candidates.
Visualize regional voting distribution.
Identify political strongholds across Ghana's regions.
Build an interactive dashboard for quick exploration and decision-making.

Key Insights

John Dramani Mahama secured the majority of valid votes with approximately 56.5%.
Mahamudu Bawumia received approximately 41.6% of total valid votes.
The Ashanti Region recorded a dominant performance for Mahamudu Bawumia.
The Volta Region overwhelmingly favored John Dramani Mahama.
Regional voting patterns reveal distinct political strongholds across the country.
Interactive filtering enables detailed regional performance analysis.
